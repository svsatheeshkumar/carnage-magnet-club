MATHCOUNTS Club

MATHCOUNTS Club at Carnage Middle School is a competitive program that promotes math achievement through solving fun & engaging problems. The tryout is scheduled for September 30th at 2:30pm in the cafeteria. Parents, please be there to pick up your child at 4:00pm. The top 28 students are chosen to participate in the club.The club meets after school once a week on Thursdays starting October 17th. The top fourteen performing students from the club will be chosen to represent Carnage Middle School.
