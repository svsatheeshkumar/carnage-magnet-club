The Carnage Middle School Student Council is a member of the NC Association of Student Councils and provides a leadership intensive program for all its members.

Club Activities

  • Students have the opportunity to attend state, district, and school leadership workshops to learn valuable life skills, such as goal setting, communication, organization, and decision making.
  • The goal of the Carnage Middle School student council is to promote school spirit and service to the community.

How to Join

The offices of president, vice-president, secretary, and treasurer are elected each spring by a ballot while the faculty advisor appoints the offices of sergeant-of-arms and historian.
