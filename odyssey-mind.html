Odyssey of the Mind

Odyssey of the Mind (OOTM) is a program that teaches students how to develop and use their creativity. Through this program, they become problem solvers and develop their confidence.

Club Activities

  • OOTM prepares students with life skills they can apply to real-life situations.
  • OOTM also emphasizes teamwork, public speaking, time management, and budgeting.

How to Join

Students who would like to be on the team should sign up with Ms. Dugger. Students of all grade levels are invited to participate.
